# Accelerators and Incubators Guide

## Tier 1 Accelerators

### Y Combinator
- **Investment**: $500k for 7% (post-money SAFE)
- **Duration**: 3 months
- **Locations**: Remote or SF
- **Acceptance**: ~1.5%
- **Companies**: 4,000+ funded
- **Apply**: ycombinator.com/apply

**What You Get**:
- $500k investment
- Weekly dinners with top founders/investors
- Demo Day (1,000+ investors)
- Lifetime network
- $100k+ in perks (AWS, GCP credits)

**Best For**: High-growth tech, ambitious founders

### Techstars
- **Investment**: $120k for 6% common stock
- **Duration**: 3 months
- **Programs**: 50+ (industry and location-specific)
- **Acceptance**: ~1-2% per program
- **Companies**: 3,000+

**What You Get**:
- $120k investment + $20k stipend
- 100+ mentors
- Curriculum and programming
- Global network

**Best For**: Industry expertise needed, geographic preferences

### 500 Global
- **Investment**: $150k for 6%
- **Duration**: 4 months
- **Focus**: Growth marketing, international
- **Acceptance**: ~2-3%

**What You Get**:
- Growth marketing expertise
- Global network (15+ countries)
- Demo Day

**Best For**: Consumer startups, international founders

## Industry-Specific Accelerators

### Rock Health (Digital Health)
- **Investment**: $100k-$150k for 4-5%
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Focus**: Digital health, medical devices
- **Location**: San Francisco

**Benefits**: Healthcare expertise, clinical validation, regulatory guidance

### IndieBio (Biotech)
- **Investment**: $250k for 8%
- **Duration**: 4 months
- **Focus**: Biotech, food tech, materials
- **Locations**: SF, NYC

**Benefits**: Fully equipped lab space, scientific mentorship

### HAX (Hardware)
- **Investment**: $120k-$250k
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Focus**: Hardware, IoT, robotics
- **Locations**: Newark (NJ), Shenzhen

**Benefits**: Prototyping facilities, manufacturing guidance

### Plug and Play (Corporate Innovation)
- **Investment**: Varies
- **Programs**: 50+ industry programs
- **Focus**: B2B enterprise, corporate partnerships

**Benefits**: Corporate pilots, enterprise customer intros

## Application Strategy

**Before Applying**:
- Research fit (stage, industry, location)
- Show traction (revenue, users, growth)
- Build team (favor teams over solo)
- Prepare materials (deck, demo)

**Improving Odds**:
- Apply to multiple programs (5-10)
- Show momentum (week-over-week growth)
- Demonstrate coachability
- Network with alumni (warm intros)
- Reapply if rejected

**Considerations**:
- Equity dilution (6-8% is significant)
- Geographic commitment (3 months)
- Timing (are you ready?)

## Alternatives

- **Studio programs**: Company builders
- **University programs**: Stanford StartX, Harvard i-lab
- **Corporate accelerators**: Industry-specific
- **Virtual accelerators**: Remote, flexible
